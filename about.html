About VitalFit

Fitness advice that respects your time.

VitalFit started with a simple frustration: most fitness content online is either selling something, or written for people who already train five days a week. Neither helped the people we knew — busy, motivated, but short on time and tired of conflicting advice.

So we built something different. Every article here is written to be usable the same day you read it. No 40-step meal plans. No workouts that require equipment you don't own. Just what actually works, explained plainly.

We update articles as the evidence changes, and we're upfront when something is a general guideline rather than a hard rule — bodies are different, and good advice leaves room for that.
